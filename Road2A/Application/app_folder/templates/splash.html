Welcome to the Splash Page

This is an online appointment system for students and tutors to schedule/approve/cancel tutoring sessions with ease through a calendar and a chat box feature.
These main features will help eliminate communication problems and save time for tutors and students.

➡Via home page you can visit the login page or the splash document by clicking on the buttons "log in!" and "Splash Page"



➡After you have clicked the Log in button, it will direct you to the login page, in this page you can enter in your password and username. Once you are done just press the button "Sign in "
➡ Suppose you dont have an account with us, you can click the 'create a new account option' on the login page and it will redirect you to that page.
➡ If you have created your account, it will redirect you to the login page, once you have logged in you can view the meetings page! In this page,it lists out your meetings.




➡ On the navigation bar, if you click on the tab "logout", you will be logged out. If you click on the splash tab, you will be able to view the splash document.



➡ Guest can view a creator availability by going to their creator page, e.g. 127.0.0.1/kart




➡ Guest can create appointments by going to a creator page.




➡ The settings page will allow the creater to set availability, meeting length, set email preference, and delete your account.




➡ In the settings page, when you press delete account, it will delete your account and direct you to this page.




➡ In the settings page, when you press the button or email verifcation, it will direct you to this page.
